About The game will begin by pressing the 'New Game' button. The first player will input their name and be given a passphrase. The other players will click 'Join Game' button (up to four players total). They will input their name and passphrase given. When all the players have joined the same game, the start button will begin the game for everyone. Each player will roll the dice by pressing the ‘Roll’ button. This will give the player five dice each (20 dice total in the pot for 4 players). The first player will make the first bid by pressing single dice face and selecting the quantity of that face they believe in the pot (total amount of dice). The next person will either increase the bid by quantity, dice face, or both or call bullshit ('Bullshit' button) on the prior bid. A player will get a mark by overbidding and getting called out, or a player will get a mark by calling out an underbid. As each player gets five marks, they will drop out of the game. Last player standing wins!
